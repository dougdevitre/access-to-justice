## Pilot at a glance

- **Duration:** 36 months
- **Counties:** St. Louis (21st Circuit), Greene (31st), Boone (13th)
- **Combined coverage:** ~24% of Missouri population, ~22% of family filings, ~14% of family judges
- **Total budget:** $1,454,510
- **Funding stack:** 20% funder cap enforced regardless of category; blended philanthropy + academic + individual + modest CoTrackPro subscription

## Budget workbook

The companion xlsx file ([13b-budget-workbook.xlsx](./13b-budget-workbook.xlsx)) is formula-driven. Change a rate or FTE in the Detail sheet; Summary and Funding Sources totals update automatically. The 20% cap is enforced via a cell that checks `=IF(MAX(funding %)>0.2, "YES — REBALANCE", "OK")`.

Four sheets:

1. **Summary** — Category × Year grid with cross-sheet links to Detail.
2. **Detail** — 16 line items across Personnel / Board / Infra / Academic / Contingency.
3. **Funding Sources** — Five funder categories with target % and secured $ tracking.
4. **Assumptions** — Ten documented assumptions with sources.

## Success criteria

The evaluation framework (document 16) defines three levels:

- **MVP:** Must all be true for pilot to be declared successful at minimum threshold.
- **Target:** Realistic success case.
- **Stretch:** Exceptional execution with favorable conditions.

Three pre-registered hypotheses are tested at Month 36 without post-hoc modification.

## Sustainability after Year 3

The pilot does NOT assume continued grant funding beyond Year 3. Two scenarios contemplated (statutory line-item vs. blended philanthropy + academic + subscription). A formal sustainability review occurs at Month 24.
